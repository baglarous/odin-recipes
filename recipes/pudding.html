Pudding

Description

My mom's best chocolate pudding recipe, made with cornstarch and cocoa.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ cup cornstarch
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¾ cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Stir together sugar, cocoa, cornstarch, and salt in a saucepan. Place over medium heat, and stir in milk. Bring to a boil, and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens enough to coat the back of a metal spoon.
  2. Remove from heat, and stir in butter and vanilla. Let cool briefly, and serve warm, or chill in the refrigerator until serving.
